Twitter PR Checker

Twitter PR Checker analyzes your tweets to determine how many inappropriate words you have tweeted so you can then determine if you need to clean up your twitter for potential employers. It then will graph out all of the users that it analyzed and based on their tweets, create a graph with the amount of inappropriate and appropriate words that you used.
